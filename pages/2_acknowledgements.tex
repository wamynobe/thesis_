\chapter*{Acknowledgements}
\addcontentsline{toc}{chapter}{Acknowledgements}

I would like to express my sincere gratitude to my supervisor,
\thesissupervisor, for the guidance, patience, and constructive feedback
provided throughout the development of this thesis.

I am deeply indebted to Dr.~Yen~Ying~Ng for sharing the Polish learner
corpus and for the careful expert annotation of both the tonal and the
phonetic dimensions of every recording. Her perceptual judgements form the
reference signal against which every model reported in this thesis is
evaluated, and the present work would not have been possible without her
contribution.

I would also like to thank Tadeusz~Brzeski and Kamil~Fischbach, whose
parallel project on the same learner corpus implemented the
phonetic-accuracy classifier~\cite{brzeski2025mprp}. Their work
complements the tonal-accuracy model developed here and made it possible
to frame this thesis as one half of a two-dimensional pronunciation
assessment pipeline rather than as a stand-alone classifier.
